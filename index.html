About Me

In 2012 I left the UK to help setup a documentary arts centre in Thailand. This was the beginning of an adventure that was to last more than four years.

I ended up living and working in Melbourne, which is where I met my now partner. During our time living in the Southern Hemisphere we were fortunate to visit Thailand, Laos, Vietnam, Cambodia, Malaysia, Indonesia, Singapore and the Philipinnes.

I am a avid photographer who has achieved a life goal of having a photograph published by The National Geographic. When I am not taking photographs I am busy learning to code whilst drinking coffee and discovering new music on Spotify.
